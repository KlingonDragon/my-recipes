# Salmon en Croûte
**Source:** My Mum

Serves|Steaming Time|Oven Time
:-:|:-:|:-:
$n$|25m|30m

## Ingredients
Item|Quantity
:--|--:
Salmon|$n$ fillets
Puff pastry¹|$\dfrac{n}{3}$ sheets
New Potatoes|250g $\times n$
Veg|100g $\times n$

¹ Note: I use ready rolled puff pastry sheets

## Method
0) Skin Salmon
0) Wrap salmon in pastry
0) Bake until golden
0) Boil water
0) Add water, potatoes, and veg to steamer
0) Steam
